FAQs

Common questions

What industries does Lens typically serve?
+
We work across fintech, healthcare, logistics, energy, and retail — typically with mid-to-large enterprises undergoing digital transformation or scaling rapidly. Our work is industry-agnostic; what matters is the complexity and ambition of the problem.
How does Lens price its services?
+
We offer project-based, retainer, and time-and-materials engagements depending on scope. All engagements begin with a discovery phase. We are transparent about costs from day one — no surprises.
What is the typical project timeline?
+
Small-to-medium projects range from 6 to 16 weeks. Larger enterprise engagements or full platform builds may run 6 to 18 months. We provide detailed milestone plans after the discovery phase so timelines are always clear and agreed upon.
Does Lens offer post-launch support?
+
Yes. Every delivered project includes a 90-day warranty period. Beyond that, we offer structured support and maintenance retainers with defined SLAs, available 24/7 for critical infrastructure clients.
Can Lens work with our in-house engineering team?
+
Absolutely. Many of our engagements are hybrid — we embed with client teams, augment capability, and transfer knowledge throughout the process. We believe in building your team's strength, not dependency on us.
Where is Lens based and do you work internationally?
+
We are headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria, with clients across West Africa, the UK, and North America. Our team operates in multiple time zones, and we have experience managing distributed, international engagements seamlessly.
